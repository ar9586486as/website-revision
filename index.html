Truly decentralized oracles are here

Blockchains do not have access to the data from outside the blockchain. Such data is necessary for operation of various decentralized applications. This problem is solved by oracles. Razor is truly decentralized oracle platform offering a high degree of decentralization and economic security, which is crucial for decentralized finance applications.

Features

Proof Of Stake
Stakers are encouraged to behave in an honest and timely manner, or they risk losing their stake.
High economic security
Every Staker in the Razor contributes to the economic security. Just like Ethereum or Bitcoin.
Truly decentralized
End-to-end decentralized oracle network with high resilience towards collusion and bribing attacks.
Onchain Governance
The first and only oracle network with onchain governance. Razor can self adapt to changing environment and protect the network from various attacks.
Scalability
Can be deployed on layer 2 solutions for practically infinite scaling.
Stability over long term
Razor can self adjust to changes in the environment and provide reliable results over long term.
